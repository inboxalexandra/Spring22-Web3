White Rose with a Twist

Whenever we hear White Rose, we automatically think of an awesome intricate merger of floral beauty and spring, brought out into the atmosphere to create a heavenly presence. The combination is black coumarin, rose de Mai, pink pepper, oakmoss, smoked gaiac, cinnamon leaf, hedione, lemon peel, cistus Spain, tuberose, and myrrh. Yes, even the three wise men had taste!
